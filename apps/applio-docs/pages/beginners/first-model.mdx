---

2. Create your dataset: [Guide on creating a dataset](/guides/create-datasets/create-datasets).

3. Learn how to easily extract vocals or audio: [Audio Isolating Guides](/guides/audio-isolating/uvr).

4. Verify the sample rate of your dataset: [Learn how to check it](/getting-started/audio-analizer).
